    About Us

    Welcome to DGS Development, your trusted partner in transforming residential spaces across Manchester and beyond. With over a decade of experience, we have built a solid reputation for excellence in residential extensions, loft conversions, and new builds.

    Who we are

    Based in the vibrant city of Manchester, DGS Development is a team of dedicated professionals passionate about bringing your home improvement dreams to life. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for homeowners looking to enhance their living spaces.

    Our expertise

    • Residential Extensions: Whether you need more room for a growing family or want to add value to your property, our expert team can design and construct seamless extensions that blend perfectly with your existing home.
    • Loft Conversions: Unlock the potential of your attic with our innovative loft conversion solutions. From cozy bedrooms to functional home offices, we transform underutilized spaces into beautiful, practical areas.
    • New Builds: Dreaming of a brand-new home? We offer bespoke new build services, ensuring that every detail is tailored to your vision. Our comprehensive approach covers everything from initial design to final finishes.

    Why choose DGS Development

    • Experience: With over 10 years in the industry, we bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every project.
    • Quality: We use only the finest materials and adhere to the highest standards of workmanship, ensuring lasting results.
    • Customer Focus: Your satisfaction is our priority. We work closely with you at every stage, offering transparent communication and personalized service.
    • Local Insight: As a Manchester-based company, we understand the local architecture and building regulations, allowing us to deliver projects that meet both your needs and the area's standards.
